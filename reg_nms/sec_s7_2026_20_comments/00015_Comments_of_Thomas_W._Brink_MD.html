Subject: File No. S7-2026-20
From: Thomas W. Brink, MD

I write to vehemently oppose the proposed regulation that would allow corporations to report financial information every six months instead of every three month. This change will allow insiders to determine how the markets will perceive corporations and profit from the knowledge that they have to the exclusion of individual investors. In other words, "The rich get richer" and the poor man suffers. This proposed rule is purely for the elite. Thomas W. Brink, MD
